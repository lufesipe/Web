Chadwick Aaron Boseman

The man who became a real-life hero

Here are some facts about Boseman's career:

  • Studied directing at Howard University, graduating in 2000 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts.
  • His first major role as a series regular was at 'Persons Unknown' (2010).
  • Boseman's breakthrough performance came as baseball player Jackie Robinson in the biographical film '42' (2013).
  • He continued to portray historical figures, starring in 'Get on Up' (2014) as singer James Brown and 'Marshall' (2017) as Supreme Court Justice Thurgood Marshall.
  • Achieved international fame for playing superhero Black Panther in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) from 2016 to 2019.
